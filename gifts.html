Products made from natural materials have always been in high demand, especially when received as a gift from a loved one. One popular natural material used to make original and luxurious gifts is amber.

Products made of this "sunny" material impress with an intricate game of tints and shades, clarity and conciseness of facets, originality and, at the same time, refinement of the appearance. Amber gift items are suitable as an exquisite and non-standard present for any occasion, such as a birthday, wedding, New Year's Day, professional holiday, etc.

Amber jewellery is presented by multitude of exclusive gifts such as: necklaces, pendants, brooches, rings and earrings.

You can find various table-ware items like: tea and coffee sets, plates, wine and punch glasses, clock movements of different configuration, souvenir figurines, decorative plates, play sets, cups, business card holders, caskets and many other things.

Amber gifts are made with the use of modern amber processing technologies, the latest equipment, experience and professional skills of the best craftsmen.
